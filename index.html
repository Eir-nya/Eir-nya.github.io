Create Your Frisk
Story time!

Around early 2016, after finishing Undertale and thoroughly enjoying myself, I stumbled across Unitale and decided - you know what, why not?
I started off strong, and very quickly became able to make tricky and hacky things, including workarounds and engine-breaking systems.
(Most of these are viewable through the archive below!)

I fiddled with the engine here and there, but eventually an upgrade, Create Your Frisk, surfaced.
It fixed a lot of problems with Unitale and made things easier to work with, however it was arguably even laggier than Unitale!
I eventually was able to start learning C# and edit the engine itself.


Before long, I'd started improving everything and adding many new quality of life improvements and features!
Create Your Frisk v0.6.2 through Create Your Frisk v0.6.5 are each about 90%-95% my work.
Things have changed since then and I've since resigned.
However, after about five years, I've also released my first battle!

I'm now turning Create Your Frisk into a fully-fledged game, Crossovertale! (See above)
